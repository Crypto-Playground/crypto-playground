Penny Jar Example dApp

This app implements a simple "penny jar". You can do two things with it:

  1. Donate Pennies. When you donate any amount of ETH, you also get to change the current message that's written on the jar.
  2. Take Pennies. You're allowed to take up to half of the pennies in the jar.

If you're looking at this on the web, rather than on your local dev box, then this jar lives on the Ropsten Test Network. Make sure MetaMask is installed, and then get some ETH for one of your Ethereum accounts by visiting a Ropsten Faucet.
